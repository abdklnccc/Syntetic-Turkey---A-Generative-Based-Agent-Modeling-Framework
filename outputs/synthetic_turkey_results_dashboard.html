Executive Reading

The run is complete and technically clean, but behaviorally over-opposition. The simulated electorate puts Kılıçdaroğlu ahead in both rounds, while the actual election favored Erdoğan. The largest model gaps are weak Erdoğan retention among ambiguous conservative/protest voters and almost no Sinan Oğan pathway.

Broadcast activity appeared in 14 event ticks. All final outputs are based on 11100 structured voter decisions.
